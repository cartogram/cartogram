East Van
This is where we stayed when we were there, it has like 20 or so breweries (some better than others). Commercial Drive reminded me of a cross between Roncesvalles and Little Italy. Lots of shops, but nothing really stood out that I can remember.

Postmark
Neat space, big door, worth having a drink here. Beer is good, not my favourite.
33 acres
Really minimal brewery and space. Good snacks, worth having a beer here.

Strange fellows
Really cool illustrations on cans, cool beer, small bottle shop/brew pup.

Crab Park
Kinda neat little beach on the north side of Vancouver. Cool to just sit with a drink and look out onto the water.

Kits Beach
Kinda yuppy area, but really nice beach. It’s a bit of a walk to get here, but very manageable and worth it. Lots of cool stuff to walk by on the way, Vanier park, etc…

Alibi room
Popular brew pub with lots of beer and snacks. We went here more than once.

Stanley Park
Plan to visit this park a few times. You can run all the way around it and trails all though it as well.

Revolver Coffee
Well known coffee shop. Kinda like one of those things that Toronto has 50 of, but worth going to if you want a really good, fancy coffee. Reminds me of early bird.

Charlee and lee
Nice jumpsuits for women. Leanna might like.
